Orion Nebula (M42)

Target: The Great Orion Nebula, a stellar nursery 1,344 light-years away, birthplace of thousands of stars.

Exposures: 2.5 hours (50x3min subs), captured during a crisp winter night.

Telescope: William Optics RedCat 51 refractor on an EQ6-R Pro mount. Canon EOS R6 modified for full-spectrum imaging.

Processing: Blended LRGB in Photoshop, with selective sharpening to highlight the Trapezium cluster.
